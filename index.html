Research Scientist

Hey, I'm Luana 👋

I'm a research scientist working at the intersection of deep reinforcement learning, robotics, and LLM alignment. My work focuses on building intelligent systems that can learn, adapt, and behave reliably in the real world.

I've collaborated with companies on applied AI R&D projects — from recommendation systems and credit recovery to natural language models for specialized domains. I'm particularly interested in bridging rigorous research with practical, deployable solutions.
